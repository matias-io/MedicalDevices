What Are Medical Devices?

Put simply, Medical Devices are a vareity of technologies that make our lives easier and is intended to help us medically. These machines are present in various ways in our lives and come in many forms. Examples of Medical devices include dialyses machine, stethescopes and pacemakers but can go as simple to tweezers or needles. Some Devices are branching out with other technologies such as prothetic arms that are now working with AI to furthur achieve efficiency. Innovation such as 3d machines capable to print bones are arising every day with more and more capabilities.
